# Prompt

What is ETH Zurich known for?

---

# Response

ETH Zurich (Eidgenössische Technische Hochschule Zürich) is widely considered one of the most prestigious and selective universities in the world. It is consistently ranked among the top five universities in continental Europe and the top ten globally.

Here are the key things ETH Zurich is known for:

### 1. The "MIT of Europe"
ETH Zurich is often compared to the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) in the United States due to its focus on engineering and natural sciences and its strong emphasis on practical applications of theoretical knowledge. It is known for producing graduates who are highly employable and innovative.

### 2. Elite Academic Rankings
In major global university rankings (such as QS World University Rankings and Times Higher Education), ETH Zurich is almost always ranked:
*   **#1 in Continental Europe**
*   **Top 10 globally** for Engineering & Technology
*   **Top 10 globally** for Natural Sciences

### 3. World-Class Architecture
ETH Zurich is the undisputed leader in architecture education in the German-speaking world. Its Department of Architecture is renowned worldwide and is often cited as one of the best architecture schools on the planet. The university’s main building (the "ETH-Hönggerberg Campus") was designed by renowned architect Marcel Breuer and is a masterpiece of modernist design.

### 4. Pioneering Physics and Mathematics
Historically, ETH Zurich has been a powerhouse in the hard sciences.
*   **Albert Einstein:** While he did not graduate from ETH (he studied at the Swiss Federal Polytechnic, the predecessor institution), he was a student there and the university maintains a strong legacy in physics.
*   **Nobel Laureates:** ETH has produced over 20 Nobel Prize winners, including more than 30 laureates affiliated with the university as students or professors. Notable names include Wernher von Braun (rocketry) and Richard R. Ernst (chemistry).

### 5. Excellence in Computer Science and Robotics
ETH Zurich is a global hub for computer science research and robotics. It is the alma mater of many successful tech entrepreneurs and has produced world-class robots (like the ballbot *Cassie* and the robotic hand *DexNet*). It is heavily involved in AI research, machine learning, and blockchain technology.

### 6. International and Multicultural
Despite being a Swiss public university, it is highly international.
*   **Language:** While Swiss German is spoken in local social circles, all lectures are held in **English**.
*   **Diversity:** Students come from over 120 different countries, making it a melting pot of cultures.

### 7. Innovation and Entrepreneurship
ETH Zurich has a very strong track record of turning research into startups. It is one of the most innovative universities in the world in terms of spin-off companies per capita. The campus has a vibrant startup ecosystem, fostering connections between students, researchers, and the industry.

**Summary:** ETH Zurich is synonymous with academic rigor, cutting-edge research in engineering and science, and producing leaders in technology and industry.
